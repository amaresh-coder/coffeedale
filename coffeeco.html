About Us

What makes our coffee special?

The coffee bean we buy, we typically don’t roast immediately, and instead age it for at least a year. Like a fine wine that gets better after some years "resting" before being consumed, we feel our coffee is softer yet bigger because we age it to reduce acids giving it a smoother taste

Our coffee stands out for its exceptional quality and unique flavor profile. From the origin to the final brew, every step in our coffee-making journey is infused with passion and expertise, making each sip of our coffee a truly special experience.
